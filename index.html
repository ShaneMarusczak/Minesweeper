Controls

  • Arrow keys: Navigate between cells
  • Enter or Space: Reveal a cell (or chord if on a number with correct flags)
  • F key: Toggle flag on a cell
  • Right-click: Cycle through flag and question mark
  • Click on revealed number: Chord (reveal neighbors if flags match number)
  • Touch: Tap to reveal, long-press to flag
